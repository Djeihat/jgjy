# Decisions

A running log of decisions made and their rationale.

---

## Household granularity
**Decision:** No real-time, household-level tracking.
**Rationale:** Requires expensive IoT/RFID infrastructure and creates
privacy/legal exposure under the CPRA. Aggregating to the census tract or
neighborhood scale is sufficient for civic impact and sidesteps those
liabilities. This mirrors the approach used by CoolClimate (UC Berkeley).

---

## Primary angle: civic engagement over real estate
**Decision:** Focus on the investigative-journalism / civic-engagement pathway.
**Rationale:** The real estate valuation angle competes directly with entrenched,
heavily capitalized players (First Street Foundation, ClimateCheck) and faces
active hostility from the real estate industry — Zillow was forced to remove
climate risk scores in late 2025 after pressure from MLS boards. The civic
engagement angle has strong historical precedent for impact (see TRI / toxic
emissions reduction) and a clearer path to a differentiated product.
